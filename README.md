# HOTEL FOOD SERVICE

# Introduction
A 2D waiter simulator where players deliver food orders to hotel clients.

# Objectives:
Identify which room the food will be served
Identify which level the food will be served
Deliver the food within the designated time frame

# Scope and Limitations
Scope:
Hotel building is the only location
You play as a waiter

Limitations:
Cannot access outside of map (hotel)
Only a waiter role is available

# What Makes It Unique
Instead of cooking from the same genre, this simple 2D game focuses on the waiter
who delivers the food to the clients.
